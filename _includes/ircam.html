IRCAM

IRCAM (Institut de Recherche et Coordination Acoustique/Musique) is a French research institution founded in the 1970s by Pierre Boulez with governmental fuding. IRCAM has been active in researching new computer music technologies, performance technologies, and human-computer interaction. As well as being a key research centre in the sciences of new music, the institution is also a hub for the dissemination of avant garde electroacoustic art music.

Further information on the IRCAM website .

Address: 1, place Igor-Stravinsky, 75004 Paris.

Métro : Hôtel de Ville, Rambuteau, Châtelet, Les Halles

Ircam is next to the famous Centre Pompidou and should be easy to find.
